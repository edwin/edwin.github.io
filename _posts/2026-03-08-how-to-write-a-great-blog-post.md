Writing consistently is hard. Here are a few principles that help me.

## 1. Start with one clear idea

Don't try to cover everything in one post. Pick one specific thing you want the reader to walk away knowing.

## 2. Write a bad first draft

Perfectionism kills momentum. Write badly, then edit. The goal of a first draft is just to exist.

## 3. Use short paragraphs

Online readers scan. Short paragraphs and clear headings make your writing much easier to consume.

## 4. End with a takeaway

What should the reader do or think now that they've finished? Give them a clear conclusion.
