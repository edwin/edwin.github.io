Welcome to my corner of the internet! This is my first blog post, and I'm excited to start writing.

## Why I started this blog

I wanted a place to document things I learn, share ideas, and improve my writing. A personal blog felt like the perfect fit.

## What to expect

I'll be writing about:
- **Tech** — tutorials, tools, and opinions
- **Projects** — things I'm building
- **Ideas** — half-baked thoughts worth exploring

## How this site is built

This blog is powered by [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) and hosted on [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/) — completely free. Posts are written in Markdown, which makes the writing experience clean and distraction-free.

Stay tuned for more posts. Thanks for reading!
